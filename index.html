About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate at Old Dominion University with a deep focus on Trustworthy AI. My research tackles the core challenges of reliability, security, and explainability in artificial intelligence, particularly within Multimodal Large Language Models. I have a passion for bridging the gap between academic theory and real-world application, demonstrated by my experience in developing and deploying full-stack AI solutions. From creating blockchain-augmented LLMs to ensure data provenance to building AI co-pilots for aviation maintenance, my goal is to create AI systems that are not only powerful but also responsible and safe.
